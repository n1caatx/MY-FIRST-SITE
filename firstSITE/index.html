What Is The Secret Of Internet

The part of the internet indexed by standard search engines like Google or Bing is known as the “visible web” or “surface web.” One estimate shows that there were approximately 4.5 billion indexed web pages at the end of 2017, and it only makes up a tiny percentage of the entire internet.
